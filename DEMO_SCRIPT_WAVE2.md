# Demo script — Mosaic, Wave 2 (90 seconds)

Target length **80–90s**. Record at 1440p / 30fps, dark UI, clean cursor, no
notifications. One continuous take is ideal; the cuts below are optional.

Before you hit record:
- Open the **landing page** in one tab and **/app** in another (pre-warmed so
  data is already loaded — no spinners on camera).
- Set system zoom so the hero preview and the dashboard fit without scrolling.
- Have the cursor ready; speak at a steady pace.

---

## 0:00–0:10 — Hook + live product preview

> **VO:** "Most retail traders never lose on the wrong coin — they lose because
> they never built a portfolio at all. Mosaic is a one-person crypto hedge fund,
> run by an agent."

**Screen:** Landing hero. Let the **self-playing product preview** run one full
loop — thesis → agent → basket → backtest & execute — under the headline. It
sells the whole product before you say another word.

## 0:10–0:22 — One sentence in, an index out

> **VO:** "It reads SoSoValue's news, ETF flows and SSI indices for the data, and
> routes execution through SoDEX's orderbook. Watch — zero setup."

**Screen:** Click **Launch App**. On the dashboard, click the **"AI
Infrastructure"** sample-thesis card. It fills the prompt, amount and risk and
submits in one click.

## 0:22–0:40 — The agent reasons, on camera

> **VO:** "The agent decomposes the thesis, scores 20-plus tokens on SoSoValue
> momentum and sentiment, applies risk caps, and walks the SoDEX orderbook for
> every leg."

**Screen:** The agent log streams Parse → Score → Weight → Route. The basket
appears with its constituents revealing one-by-one — weight bar, per-token
rationale, risk score, "vs AI.ssi" benchmark badge.

## 0:40–0:55 — Quantify it before you risk it

> **VO:** "Before any irreversible move, Mosaic shows you what this portfolio will
> actually do — a 90-day backtest, a thousand-path Monte Carlo with value-at-risk,
> and three historical crash replays."

**Screen:** Scroll through Backtest → Monte Carlo → Stress tests. Hover one **(?)
icon** to show the plain-English explanation popping up. (Clarity beat.)

## 0:55–1:10 — Execute on SoDEX, with a confirm gate

> **VO:** "Execution is multi-leg, IOC-only, with a slippage cap and an explicit
> confirm. No market orders, no surprises."

**Screen:** Open the execution plan. Point at the **estimated slippage in bps**
and the per-leg table. Click **Review & confirm → Confirm & execute**. The fill
animates; the basket lands under **My baskets** and auto-expands with every fill.

## 1:10–1:22 — It persists, and it's honest

> **VO:** "Baskets persist — revisit and the realised-versus-thesis curve is
> filling in. And every integration is labelled: here's exactly what's live
> versus simulated."

**Screen:** Quick cut to the **/judges** page (or **/diag**) showing the live
SoSoValue + SoDEX status and the live-vs-simulated table.

## 1:22–1:30 — Close

> **VO:** "Mosaic. Plain English in, an on-chain index out — built on SoSoValue
> and SoDEX."

**Screen:** Back to the landing hero, fade to logo + URL.

## Capture checklist (B-roll, if you want inserts)

- Close-up of the agent log streaming with sub-second timestamps.
- A second sample thesis (e.g. **Memecoins, aggressive**) to prove baskets differ.
- The (?) tooltip on Sharpe or VaR, fully expanded.
- The **/diag** page with green "reachable" badges.
- Footer URLs: sosovalue.com · sodex.com · ssi.sosovalue.com.

## Do / don't

- **Do** keep it one continuous flow if you can — judges reward a working demo.
- **Do** let the hero preview loop once; it does a lot of narrative work for free.
- **Don't** connect a real wallet on camera; the simulated testnet flow is the
  point, and the /judges page explains why.
- **Don't** linger on any one panel more than ~4 seconds.
