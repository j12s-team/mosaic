# Demo video script — Mosaic (90 seconds)

Target length: **75–90 seconds.** Shoot at 1440p, 30fps, dark UI, clean cursor.

## Cold open (0–8s)

> **VO**: "Most retail traders don't lose money picking the wrong coin. They lose
> because they never built a portfolio in the first place."

**Visual:** Mosaic landing hero — the "Your personal crypto hedge fund, run by an agent" headline, slow zoom-in.

## The pitch (8–18s)

> **VO**: "Mosaic turns one sentence into a thematic on-chain index — using SoSoValue
> for the data and SoDEX for the execution."

**Visual:** Cut to "How it works" four-step strip on the landing page, panning right.

## Live data proof (18–28s)

> **VO**: "Real signals from day one. Here's the SoSoValue news feed, ETF flows,
> and the MAG7 SSI composition, all loaded server-side."

**Visual:** "Powered by real data" section, hover over the 3 cards. Optionally cut to the network tab to show `/api/v1/news/featured/currency` and `/api/v1/etf/spot/eth/flow`.

## Build a basket (28–48s)

> **VO**: "I describe what I want — AI-infrastructure exposure with a thousand bucks,
> moderate risk. The agent decomposes the thesis, scores candidates against SoSoValue
> momentum, sentiment and liquidity, and applies risk-aware weights with a
> concentration cap."

**Visual:** Click "Launch app", paste the preset thesis, hit "Build my basket". Show the agent log streaming. Show the resulting basket — gradient weight bar, per-token rationale, risk score, benchmark badge ("vs AI.ssi").

## Execute on SoDEX (48–62s)

> **VO**: "Mosaic walks SoDEX's orderbook for every leg, simulates the IOC fill,
> and only then asks me to confirm. No market orders, no slippage leak."

**Visual:** Scroll to execution plan card. Highlight the "47 bps" total estimated slippage and the per-leg table. Click "Review & confirm" → confirmation panel → "Confirm & execute" → fill animation → "Basket executed".

## Maintain (62–80s)

> **VO**: "And the loop keeps running. When SoSoValue news flagged a Bittensor
> emissions catalyst — Mosaic flagged it back, with citations, and proposed a
> rebalance. I approve in one click."

**Visual:** Scroll to portfolio. Highlight the rebalance proposal card — show the "Increase TAO, trim RNDR" diff and the citations chips. Click "Approve & route to SoDEX".

## Close (80–90s)

> **VO**: "Mosaic. Plain English in. On-chain index out. Built on SoSoValue and SoDEX
> for the Buildathon."

**Visual:** Cut back to landing hero, fade to logo + URL.
